Communications

With advanced photo markup, unlimited group chat capacity, document attachment compatibility with all file types, and line order generation directly from texts, communication has never been easier. Bring power to your communications, making each text a possible tool to begin building off of.

Timeline

On top of timelines automatically populating in the in-app calendar and in Google calendar, users can also adjust project durations and leave informative comments to increase transparency. Cornerstone will pull dates form your texts to suggest project timelines in a Gantt chart formation. These timelines can be adjusted to accommodate for any set-back or expedition.

Finanical Tracking

Enjoy financial clarity with our easy to use payment tracking software, seeing just how much of the job has been compensated. We have integrated with QuickBooks to make accounting and billing easier than ever. We’re proud to feature DocuSign compatability, guaranteeing all parties involved are well educated regarding their project’s scope.

Punch List

Pull your clients requests into your line or change orders instantly, never missing a beat with client communication and ensuring your subs and architects have the most up-to-date information. Skip the change-order paperwork while remaining incredibly organized with your projects. Users can even seamlessly craft punch lists and delineate daily tasks to teams using data directly pulled from their communication and line order channels.

Invoice

With our bespoke billing and invoicing system, you can make and receive payments with confidence and never have to worry about client disputes. Clients can pay their contractors via PayPal, credit card, e-check, and even carrier pigeon. Contractors have the same access to our payment systems when it comes to paying their subs, making employee payment easier than ever. Users benefit from no transfer fees, payment clarity, and instant deposits.

Order Creation

Apart from being pulled directly from communications,  orders can also be created in project-specific workbooks. Users can quickly tack down project notes, then go back later to create orders from them, or they can almost instantly generate line orders while in the field. All it takes is a quick a job description and price estimation, then Cornerstone will handle the rest for you, automatically generating punch-lists, invoices, and notifying clients of the new tasks.
